项目简介

CoinRealm 项目背景与定位

CoinRealm 是面向普通大众的轻量化Web3任务生态平台，降低区块链与加密货币参与门槛。依托任务激励、双层推广、代币挖矿体系，让无区块链基础的用户可以日常轻松获取加密收益，打造普惠型加密流量生态。

Project Introduction

Background & Positioning of CoinRealm

CoinRealm is a lightweight Web3 task ecosystem platform for the general public, lowering the entry barrier of blockchain and cryptocurrency. Relying on task incentives, two-tier referral system and token mining mechanism, users without blockchain experience can gain crypto earnings easily every day, building an inclusive crypto traffic ecosystem.

核心功能详解

四大核心板块

  • 任务系统：各类日常线上任务发布、领取、审核、结算一站式服务
  • 邀请机制：一级直推、二级间推双层返佣，好友完成任务即可持续获得分红
  • 每日空投：每日完成任意任务，即可领取平台原生代币CRLM空投奖励
  • 挖矿系统：持有CRLM代币开启持币生息挖矿，获取持续被动收益

Core Functions Details

Four Core Modules

  • Task System: One-stop service for publishing, claiming, reviewing and settling all kinds of daily online tasks
  • Referral Mechanism: Two-tier commission for direct & indirect invites, earn dividends whenever your referrals complete tasks
  • Daily Airdrop: Claim native CRLM token airdrop rewards daily after finishing any task
  • Mining System: Hold CRLM tokens to activate staking mining and gain steady passive income

代币经济（CRLM）

代币总量

CRLM代币总发行量为1亿枚，总量恒定，永不增发

代币分配

  • 用户空投&任务挖矿：最大占比，面向全体参与用户
  • 社区生态运营：用于活动、推广、社群激励
  • 团队储备：锁仓分期释放，保障平台长期运维
  • 市场流动性：cex/dex上币与交易流动性供给

代币用途

  • 日常任务奖励结算
  • 提现渠道：CRLM提现，未来支持USDT双向提现
  • 链内转账、交易手续费抵扣
  • 质押挖矿获取收益

释放机制

用户挖矿与空投即时释放；团队、运营份额按月线性解锁，杜绝大额砸盘，稳定代币价格。

Token Economics (CRLM)

Total Token Supply

The total supply of CRLM is 100,000,000 tokens with a fixed cap, no additional minting forever.

Token Allocation

  • User Airdrop & Task Mining: Largest share for all participants
  • Community Operation: For campaigns, promotion and community incentives
  • Team Reserve: Locked and vested gradually to guarantee long-term platform operation
  • Market Liquidity: For CEX & DEX listing and trading liquidity provision

Token Utilities

  • Settlement reward for daily tasks
  • Withdrawal: CRLM withdrawal available, USDT withdrawal will be supported in the future
  • Deduction for on-chain transfer and trading fees
  • Stake tokens to earn mining rewards

Vesting Mechanism

Mining and airdrop tokens are released instantly; team & operation portions are unlocked linearly month by month to avoid massive dump and stabilize token price.

平台收益来源

三大盈利渠道

  • 广告收入：品牌方任务投放、站内流量广告收益
  • 交易手续费：代币提现、转账、质押赎回手续费分成
  • 增值服务：高阶权益、专属任务等付费增值服务

Platform Revenue Sources

Three Profit Channels

  • Advertising Revenue: Brand task orders and in-site display ads
  • Transaction Fees: Commission from withdrawal, transfer and unstaking fees
  • Value-added Services: Premium membership, exclusive paid tasks and other privileges

用户赚取路径

从注册到提现完整流程

注册绑定钱包 → 领取日常任务完成，赚取CRLM积分奖励；积分可在社区内流通，用于发布任务、协作、交换价值；CRLM作为数字积分具备加密资产潜力，未来或可合规流通；同时邀请好友赚取两级分红 → 持币挖矿增值 → 申请CRLM提现（未来支持USDT）至个人钱包

User Earning Path

Full Process From Registration to Withdrawal

Register & bind wallet → Complete daily tasks to earn CRLM point rewards; Points can circulate within the community for task posting, collaboration and value exchange; As digital points, CRLM holds crypto asset potential and may achieve compliant circulation in the future. Meanwhile, invite friends to gain two-tier commissions → Hold tokens for mining appreciation → Apply to withdraw CRLM (USDT will be supported later) to personal wallet
