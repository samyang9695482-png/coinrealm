新手入门

如何注册账号？

CoinRealm 支持通过加密钱包直接登录，无需手机号、邮箱注册。点击连接钱包，授权签名即可一键进入平台。

如何绑定钱包？

首页点击「连接钱包」，选择你的钱包类型完成授权绑定，钱包地址将作为你的唯一平台账户身份。

如何完成第一个任务？

进入任务大厅领取简单任务，按照要求操作完毕后提交凭证，审核通过就能领取CRLM奖励。

Getting Started

How to register?

Login directly via crypto wallet without phone/email registration. Connect wallet and sign to access the platform.

How to bind wallet?

Click Connect Wallet on homepage, select your wallet and authorize binding. Wallet address is your unique account ID.

How to finish first task?

Claim easy tasks in task hall, submit proof after completion, get CRLM rewards once approved.

任务相关

如何领取任务？

登录后打开任务大厅，浏览所有可参与任务，查看奖励与规则后点击领取即可。

如何提交凭证？

任务完成后进入任务详情页，上传截图、交易哈希等材料提交审核。

任务审核需要多久？

普通任务5-30分钟；链上交互类任务1~2小时。

任务被驳回怎么办？

查看驳回原因，补齐材料重新提交；有异议可通过社群联系客服申诉。

Tasks Guide

How to claim tasks?

Enter task hall after login, check rewards & rules then claim available tasks.

How to submit proof?

Upload screenshots, tx hash or other materials on task detail page for review.

Review duration?

5-30 mins for normal tasks; 1-2 hours for on-chain interaction tasks.

Task rejected solution?

Check rejection reason, resubmit materials or appeal via official community support.

邀请奖励

如何邀请好友？

个人中心复制专属邀请链接/邀请码，分享出去，好友通过链接注册即绑定上下级关系。

一级奖励和二级奖励有什么区别？

一级：直推好友完成一个任务后产生奖励；二级：好友邀请的下级用户完成一个任务后产生奖励，两级收益均可领取。

邀请奖励多久到账？

下级任务审核通过后，奖励实时发放至你的资产账户。

Referral Rewards

Invite friends steps?

Copy your exclusive referral link in profile, share it, friends register via link to bind relationship.

Difference between L1 & L2 commission?

Level 1: You get rewards when your direct friends complete a task; Level 2: You receive rewards when users invited by your subordinates finish tasks. Earnings from both tiers are withdrawable.

Rewards arrival time?

Funds will be credited instantly once your subordinate's task is approved.

空投与挖矿

什么是每日空投？

平台每日活跃福利，满足活跃度条件即可免费领取CRLM代币。

如何解锁空投资格？

每日完成任意任务即可解锁当日空投资格。

挖矿收益如何计算？

根据持有CRLM数量、质押时长计算，持仓越多、质押越久每日挖矿收益越高。

Airdrop & Mining

What is daily airdrop?

Daily activity bonus, you can claim free CRLM tokens after meeting activity requirements.

How to unlock airdrop eligibility?

Complete any daily task to unlock the daily airdrop qualification.

How mining rewards are calculated?

Rewards are calculated based on your CRLM holdings and staking period. More tokens and longer staking time bring higher daily income.

提现与充币

如何提现 CRLM？

资产页面选择CRLM，填写接收钱包地址、提现数量，提交提现申请。

提现需要多久？

平台审核秒过，最终到账速度取决于区块链网络拥堵状态。

充币地址在哪里？

资产页点击充币，即可查看专属充币收款地址，务必核对公链网络一致。

Deposit & Withdraw

How to withdraw CRLM?

Go to Assets page, fill in receiving wallet address and amount, then submit withdrawal application.

How long does withdrawal take?

Platform review is instant; arrival time depends on blockchain network congestion.

Where can I get deposit address?

Click Deposit on Assets page to get your exclusive deposit address, confirm the blockchain network before transferring funds.

常见问题（FAQ）

为什么我的任务显示“已结束”？

任务名额耗尽、有效期截止或发布方提前关闭任务，可选择其他未完结任务参与。

账号被锁定了怎么办？

多开刷奖励、作弊违规会触发账号冻结，前往官方社群提交申诉工单等待人工审核。

遇到问题如何联系客服？

可通过X推特、Telegram社群、Discord服务器随时联系官方客服。

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the task show ended?

The task quota is full, expired, or closed in advance by the publisher. You can choose other ongoing tasks.

What to do if my account is locked?

Accounts will be frozen for multi-account farming and cheating. Submit an appeal ticket in official communities for manual review.

How to contact customer service?

You can reach official support via X, Telegram Group and Discord Server anytime.
