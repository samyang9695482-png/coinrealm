项目简介

CoinRealm 是什么？一句话说清楚

CoinRealm是一款零门槛Web3任务生态平台，用户通过完成各类简单日常任务即可轻松获取加密资产收益。

Project Introduction

What is CoinRealm? Brief introduction

CoinRealm is a zero-threshold Web3 task ecosystem platform where users can easily earn crypto income by completing all kinds of simple daily tasks.

平台使命

我们的核心使命

让每个人都能通过简单任务赚取加密货币

Platform Mission

Our Core Mission

Enable everyone to earn cryptocurrency through simple tasks

核心功能

平台四大核心模块

  • 任务系统：支持任务发布、领取、审核与完成全流程
  • 邀请奖励：一级、二级双层推广返佣机制
  • 每日空投：完成任意任务后，每日均可领取平台代币空投
  • 挖矿系统：持有平台代币享受持币生息挖矿收益

Core Functions

Four core modules of the platform

  • Task System: Full process including task release, claim, review and completion
  • Referral Bonus: Two-tier commission system for level 1 and level 2 invites
  • Daily Airdrop: Claim platform token airdrop every day after finishing tasks
  • Mining System: Earn passive staking rewards by holding platform tokens

我们的优势

平台三大核心亮点

  • 零门槛：仅需一部手机，无需专业区块链知识即可参与
  • 即时提现：支持USDT、CRLM两种币种随时提现
  • 社区驱动：由全体用户共同自治，规则透明、收益公平

Our Advantages

Three major highlights of our platform

  • Zero threshold: Only a mobile phone is needed, no professional blockchain knowledge required
  • Instant withdrawal: Support USDT and CRLM withdrawal at any time
  • Community-driven: Governed by all users with transparent rules and fair earnings
